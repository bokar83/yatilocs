Secrets of the Locsmith

Proven guidance for nurturing your crown from the inside out ‚Äî because real artistry begins with real care.

Moisture Is the Move

Hydrate your locs regularly with water-based sprays and seal in with natural oils like jojoba or castor.

Sleep Smart

Wrap your hair at night in a satin or silk scarf to reduce friction and retain moisture.

Clean, But Don't Strip

Use sulfate-free shampoos that cleanse without drying. Avoid over-washing ‚Äî 1-2 times per week is often ideal.

Hands Off!

Avoid excessive twisting or styling to prevent tension and breakage around roots and edges.
